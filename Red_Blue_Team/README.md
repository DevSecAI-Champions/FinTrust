# Red Team vs Blue Team — Attack & Defend simulation

Exercise materials for the FinTrust Security Champions **Attack & Defend** simulation. The group splits into Red Team (attackers) and Blue Team (defenders). Each team uses draw.io to map their strategy, then everyone reconvenes to see who succeeded.

**Slide reference:** Session 3 — Red Team vs Blue Team (exercise files: `/Red_Blue_Team/` in the FinTrust repo).

---

## Files in this folder

| File | Team | Contents |
|------|------|----------|
| **RED_TEAM.md** | Red Team | Full attacker brief, 10 attacker types, 11 attack methods, draw.io attack map instructions |
| **BLUE_TEAM.md** | Blue Team | Full defender brief, list of 16 security controls, control selection rules, draw.io defence map instructions |
| **APPS.md** | Both | Descriptions of all three FinTrust apps (Web Frontend, Mobile App, Partner API) |
| **DEBRIEF.md** | Facilitator | Reveal script, scoring rubric, discussion prompts |
| **DRAWIO_TEMPLATES.md** | Both | Instructions and templates for draw.io attack/defence maps |

Each file is written to be read independently. Teams should not share files until the reveal.

---

## Quick start

```bash
git clone https://github.com/DevSecAI-Champions/FinTrust.git
cd FinTrust/Red_Blue_Team/
```

- **Red Team:** Open RED_TEAM.md and APPS.md. Make your selections, then map your attack in draw.io (see DRAWIO_TEMPLATES.md).
- **Blue Team:** Open BLUE_TEAM.md and APPS.md. Pick your 3 controls, then map your defence in draw.io (see DRAWIO_TEMPLATES.md).
- **Facilitator:** Use DEBRIEF.md for the reveal and scoring.

**Time:** ~20 minutes to plan and map; then reconvene for the debrief.
